About LogoD

LogoD is a solo-run logo design service founded by Sergey Svyatodukh, a passionate graphic designer with over 5 years of experience in branding and visual identity creation. Based in Ukraine, LogoD serves clients globally, delivering custom logos that reflect the unique essence of each business.

Specializing in a variety of styles—minimalist, retro, modern, and 3D—LogoD uses industry-standard tools like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to craft high-quality designs. The mission of LogoD is to empower businesses with memorable and professional logos that enhance brand recognition.

Sergey works closely with clients to understand their vision, ensuring each logo is tailored to their specific needs. LogoD has been featured on platforms like Fiverr, where Sergey has earned a reputation for reliability and creativity.

Our Design Process

1. Consultation

Clients share their project details, brand vision, and preferences via email, Telegram, or Fiverr. Sergey asks clarifying questions to align with the client's goals.

2. Concept Development

Based on the brief, Sergey creates multiple logo concepts, exploring different styles and ideas to suit the brand's identity.

3. Finalization & Delivery

After client feedback and revisions, Sergey finalizes the logo and delivers it in multiple formats (JPG, PNG, PSD, SVG, AI) for versatile use.
